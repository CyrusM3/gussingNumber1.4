Instruction

Let's play a little game! The computer is going to randomly select an integer from 1 to 50.

You'll keep guessing numbers until you find the computer's number, and the computer will tell you each time if your guess was too high or too low.

If you guess a number, the number replaces with a question mark in the small circle and sphere's border turns green and rotates 360 degrees. You can reset the game by clicking on 'Play Again!' button. Good Luck!
